About Mystori

Engineering with a human ear.

Mystori Audio is a small independent studio focused on research, design and manufacturing of high-fidelity in-ear monitors. We combine technical tools – such as measurement rigs and simulation – with careful listening, because real music and real ears do not behave like idealised graphs.

  • • Neutral yet musical tuning, built around realistic timbre.
  • • Carefully selected drivers and acoustic structures for each project.
  • • Industrial design and packaging that match the sound philosophy.
  • • OEM / ODM collaboration for partners who value the same approach.
